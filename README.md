# Business Sales Performance Analytics

## Project Overview

This project analyzes Superstore sales data to identify revenue trends, top-performing products, category performance, and regional sales distribution using Power BI and Python.

## Tools Used

* Python
* Pandas
* Matplotlib
* Power BI
* VS Code

## Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

* Total Sales: $2.30M
* Total Profit: $286.40K
* Total Orders: 5,009

## Key Insights

1. The West region generated the highest sales among all regions.
2. Technology was the best-performing category, contributing approximately 36.4% of total sales.
3. The Canon imageCLASS 2200 Advanced Copier was the top-selling product.
4. Sales showed a positive growth trend over the observed period.
5. The South region recorded the lowest sales and may require additional business attention.

## Recommendations

1. Increase marketing efforts in high-performing regions.
2. Expand product offerings in the Technology category.
3. Focus inventory planning around top-selling products.
4. Investigate strategies to improve sales performance in the South region.

## Conclusion

The analysis highlights strong sales performance in the Technology category and the West region. The dashboard provides actionable insights that can support business growth and strategic decision-making.
